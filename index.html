The Case for This

You probably already have some kind of network. What you don't have is a room of people who can look at your actual decisions in real time, tell you what's wrong, and then check whether you did anything about it next week.

Associate with people who are likely to improve you.

— Seneca

The Collective is built differently. Every seat requires active skin in the game. Every meeting has a defined structure that forces progress. Every commitment is binary — it's either done or not done. And the standards are actually enforced.

The result is compounding. After 90 days in a room like this, your decision speed increases, your blind spots shrink, and your execution rate climbs — because bad excuses don't survive the room.

Without This Group
  • Important decisions made in isolation or with people who won't give honest feedback
  • No external accountability. Goals and deadlines slip by with no consequences
  • Energy spent on the wrong thing because no one called it out or told you it was a terrible idea
With This Group
  • Decisions pressure-tested weekly by peers with real stakes
  • Public commitments with hard binary outcomes
  • Easy access to people in similar situations
  • Problems are easier to solve through group collaboration
  • Every week ends with one clear, measurable next action and accountability for whether you complete it or not
Mission

A small, private group of active operators and investors who meet weekly to increase each other's execution speed, sharpen decision quality, and enforce the commitments they make to themselves.

Weekly Meeting Structure · 60–75 min
01
Scoreboard

Last week's commitment: Done or Not Done. No discussion, no context. The record speaks for itself.

02
Status + Bottleneck

Each person covers two things in order:

  • Go over last week's commitment and show proof of completion. If no completion, explain why and what will be fixed this coming week to complete it.
  • Current focus in your business or project and any problems you're running into.
03
Commitment Lock-In

One to three concrete, measurable actions for the coming week. Binary, measurable outcomes only. The group can and will veto anything soft or untestable.

Non-Negotiable Standards
Attendance
  • Miss 2 meetings in any 8-week window → removed
  • Arrive late to 2 meetings in any 8-week window → removed
No exceptions. No votes.
Execution
  • 2 missed commitments in any 8-week window → removed
No exceptions. No votes.
Confidentiality
  • Everything discussed is fully confidential
  • One violation regardless of intent or severity results in immediate removal
Trust is the product. Protect it absolutely.
Membership
  • Every member must be actively building a business or in the process of acquiring a business or deploying capital into something
  • Advisors, observers, and consultants are not eligible
Skin in the game is a prerequisite.
Enforcement
  • No voting, no group debate, no appeals process
The group exists because the standards exist.
